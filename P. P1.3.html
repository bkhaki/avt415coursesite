User Scenario Mapping

Lily is a first time home buyer in her early 20s that loves plants and interior design. Shes grown tired of the boring planters that are offered at her local plant nursery and wants some that are as interesting as the rest of her decor and compliment each of her plants

Actor - Lily
Motivator - Wanting to add style to her new home.
Intention/Intent - Buy unique decor to work seamlessly with her plants.
Action - Use the price, size, and color filters to find the planter that best compliements each of her plants.
Resolution - Lily finds unique and stylish planters and places her order.
